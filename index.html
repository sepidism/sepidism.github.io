Sepideh Maleki

AI Postdoc Fellow

Genentech

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Genentech. My research is conducted within the department of BRAID, which specializes in applying machine learning to research biology. I am part of the "Foundational ML Research" team, where I have led and contributed to multiple research projects. My research focuses on the convergence of foundation models, graph theory, and machine learning, with a special emphasis on advancing biological and drug discovery applications. I am particularly engaged in developing machine learning methods that efficiently leverage foundation models—such as large language models and single-cell models—to tackle complex challenges in molecular and genetic perturbation. Before joining Genentech, I was a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin under supervision of Prof. Keshav Pingali.
